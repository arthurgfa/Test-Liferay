# How to Use a DVR

## Introduction

This guide covers two basic DVR tasks: controlling playback and recording TV shows.

## Section 1 – DVR

DVR controls let you pause, resume, skip ahead, or go back while watching TV or recordings.

Rewind, Play/Pause, Fast-forward, Stop

How to use:

1. Press **Play** to start or resume.  
2. Press **Pause** to stop temporarily.  
3. Use **Fast-forward** to skip ahead.  
4. Use **Rewind** to go back.  
5. Press **Stop** to exit playback.

## Section 2 – Recording a TV Show

Recording saves a show so you can watch it later.

TV show → Record → Saved

How to record:

- Live TV: Press **Record** while watching the show.  
- From the TV Guide: Open the TV guide, select a show if he is not on TV yet, and press **Record**.
